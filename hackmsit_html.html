Student - Teacher Grievances Portal

Select your option


  • For the students

    • Students will be able to voice their grievances regarding their respective schools as well as the education system. Their information will be kept confidential.

  • For the teachers

    • The teachers will be able to put forward their suggestions and complaints as well regarding pay hike, education for students etc.

  • Suggestions

    • People can give suggestions if they want to.






    Making education better

    This portal helps the government to work on the flaws existing in our education system as suggested by the students and the teachers in order to ensure quality education in schools.

    Confidentiality

    The information of the user will be kept confidential so that users don't hesitate in submitting their grievances.

    Progress graph

    The progress related to how much work has been done and how much is left will be transparent to the users so that they can keep a track.
